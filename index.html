Movie Review













Synopsis Cast and Characters Review
Po is a Panda who works in his father's noodle shop. However, he dreams of becoming a Kung Fu warrior. When he learns that the village is hosting a tournament to nominate the Dragon Warrior, he goes to the Jade Palace to watch the festivities. Little did he know that his destiny would change from there on. Jack Black (Po), Ian Mcshane (Tai Lung), Angelina Jolie (Tigress), Jackie Chan (Monkey), Lucy Liu (Viper), David Cross (Crane), Seth Rogen (Mantis) This movie got me into the Kung Fu Panda franchise. I really loved the story telling and the beautiful animation and scenery.
Now the Dragon Warrior, Po protects the Valley of Peace alongside his the Furious Five. A villain threatens Po's life and plans to use a secret weapon to wipe out the martial art and conquer China. Po must recall his past and unlock secrets of his mysterious origins; only then will he find the strength to vanquish his foe. Jack Black (Po), Angelina Jolie (Tigress), Jackie Chan (Monkey), Lucy Liu (Viper), David Cross (Crane), Seth Rogen (Mantis), Gary Oldman (Lord Shen) This was my favourite movie in the trilogy. The character writing was impressive and the plot twist at the end was unexpected. I also learned that to love the present one must first learn to understand one's past.
Po now realizes that he has a lot to learn. After reuniting with his long-lost father hr must transition from student to teacher and train a group of fun-loving, clumsy pandas to become martial-arts fighters. Together, the kung-fu brethren unite to take on the evil Kai who is a supernatural warrior who becomes stronger with each battle. Jack Black (Po), Angelina Jolie (Tigress), Jackie Chan (Monkey), Lucy Liu (Viper), David Cross (Crane), Seth Rogen (Mantis), Bryan Cranston (Panda Dad), Kate Hudson (Mei Mei) In my opinion, Kung fu Panda 3 was the least memorable. I wish it had more action and drama and I wish the villain had more depth. I had high expectations from this movie since it was the last of the trilogy. But, I do like the lessons about family that it taught me. However, the plot was bland and the movie lacked the action that I was used to in the previous movies. I did like the new places and the new characters that were introduced in Kung Fu Panda 3. These characters include Po's brothers, sisters, and his father.
